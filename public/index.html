Privacy First

No login required. The utility queries the Instagram API inside your own active tab, keeping credentials fully secure.

Sequential Bulk Unfollow

Select multiple accounts and unfollow them sequentially with configurable human-like delays to bypass Instagram's spam detection.

Local Caching

Stores scanned data directly in your browser's local storage. Re-open the overlay to immediately analyze cached lists without re-scanning.

Custom Layouts

Toggle between a high-fidelity detailed List View or a compact Grid View to browse unfollowers based on your preference.

Data Exports

Anti-Block Controls

Monitors HTTP status responses and instantly halts executions if Instagram issues a 429 rate limit to safeguard your account.

How to Run It

Step 01 Load Bookmarklet

Drag the Unfollowers Bookmarklet button to your browser's Bookmarks bar (press Ctrl+Shift+B or Cmd+Shift+B on Mac if hidden).

Step 02 Open Instagram

Open a tab, go to instagram.com, and ensure you are logged into your profile.

Step 03 Inject Overlay

Click the bookmarklet link in your bookmarks bar. The custom overlay modal will immediately slide onto the page.

Rate Limit Warning & Safe Use Guidelines

Executing API requests quickly may result in temporary blocks from Instagram. We recommend executing a scan no more than once every 10-15 minutes. During bulk unfollow operations, the script uses a 2-second rate-limiting delay, which automatically pauses if Instagram flags the requests. Avoid running multiple active sessions.

Console Warnings (Safely Ignore)

If you inspect your browser console on instagram.com, you might see pre-existing warning messages such as "ignoring invalid endpoint URL '/security/coop_report/'" or rejected Meta cookies (e.g. "th_eu_pref"). These warnings are generated natively by Instagram's own servers and will appear even before this utility is executed. They are completely normal and safe to ignore.
